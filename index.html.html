Historic Hills & Alfama
Where Lisbon's story began

Wind through the medieval labyrinth of Alfama — the oldest neighbourhood in the city. Visit the São Jorge Castle viewpoint for panoramic views that will take your breath away. Discover where fado music was born, wander through Mouraria's multicultural streets, and hear stories of Moorish conquests, earthquakes, and resilience.

UNESCO Heritage & the world's best pastry

Journey to the riverfront district where Portugal's great explorers set sail. Stand before the mighty Jerónimos Monastery and the iconic Tower of Belém, visit the Monument to the Discoveries, and taste the original pastéis de Belém where they've been baked since 1837.
